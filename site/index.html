Portfolio Plan

I am creating this portfolio to showcase my experience and projects to the viewer.

Who am I as a designer?

I am a functional and minimalistic designer. I believe that the user's interactions with software should be seamless and natural. I design user interfaces that are simple, comprehensible, and easy to navigate.

What is the most important thing I want people to take away from viewing my portfolio?

I want the viewer to take away key information regarding my experience, projects, and the skills that were involved with my projects. When applying to jobs, you are only able to get a few seconds of the recruiter's attention when looking over a resume or portfolio. Therefore, I went with a simple and organized design. My portfolio is grouped into simple sections that are easily accessible. All my information regarding experience, projects, and skills are to the point and they do not involve a lot of reading. I want the view to be able to look over the portfolio in under a minute and understand who I am as a student and professional.

What are my three strongest skills?

I believe my three strongest skills include:

  1. Technical Skills (Coding, Designing, Implementation)
  2. Organization
  3. Time Management

What am I using for inspiration?

I am using fellow Computer Science students and Software Engineers' portfolios for inspiration. I am drawn to user interfaces and portfolios that have simple and organized. Portfolios I took direct inspiration from are:
